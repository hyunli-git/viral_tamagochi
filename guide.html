üéÆ Actions Guide

üçñ Food

Feeds your pet a meal. Increases Hunger by 30%, but slightly decreases Cleanliness. Gives 10 EXP.

üç¨ Snack

Gives your pet a treat. Increases Happiness by 20%, but adds more weight (+2kg). Be careful - overweight pets can get sick!

üéÆ Game

Play the Jump Game mini-game! Press SPACE or tap to jump over obstacles. Your score determines rewards:

  • Happiness boost (up to +30%)
  • EXP gain (up to +20)
  • Weight loss (-1kg)
  • Energy cost (-15%)

üí° Light

Toggle the lights on/off. When lights are off, your pet sleeps and recovers Energy (+5% every 5 seconds).

üßπ Clean

Cleans your pet and removes any poop. Increases Cleanliness by 40%. Gives 5 EXP.

üíä Medicine

Cures your sick pet. Only works when the üíÄ icon is active. Note: Medicine tastes bad, so Happiness decreases slightly.

üëç Praise

Praises your pet for being good. Increases Discipline (+10%) and Happiness (+5%). Gives 5 EXP.

üåü Evolution System

üí° Tip: Earn EXP by feeding, playing games, cleaning, and praising your pet. The more you interact, the faster it evolves!

üíæ Saving

Your game saves automatically! Your progress is stored in your browser's local storage. If you clear your browser data, your pet will be lost.

üîÑ Reset

Want to start over? Click the Reset button to delete all data and start with a new egg.

‚ùì FAQ

Why did my pet die?

Pets die when Hunger or Happiness reaches 0%, or sometimes from untreated sickness. Make sure to check on your pet regularly!

How do I cure sickness?

When the üíÄ icon appears, your pet is sick. Click the üíä Medicine button to cure it.

Why can't I play the game?

You need at least 20% Energy to play the mini-game. Let your pet sleep first!

Does my pet need care when I'm away?

Yes! Time passes even when the game is closed. If you're away for too long, your pet's stats will decrease.
