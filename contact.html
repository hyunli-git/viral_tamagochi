Frequently Asked Questions

Is Tomo really free?

Yes! Tomo is completely free to play. We may show ads to support the game's development, but there are no hidden fees or in-app purchases.

Can I play on my phone?

Absolutely! Tomo is designed to work on any device with a web browser - desktop, tablet, or mobile phone.

My pet died. Can I get it back?

Unfortunately, when a pet dies, it cannot be revived. You'll need to start over with a new egg using the Reset button. Take better care of your next pet!

Is my data safe?

Your game data is stored locally in your browser. We don't collect or store any personal information on our servers. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

How can I support Tomo?

The best way to support us is to share Tomo with your friends! You can also help by providing feedback and suggestions.

Response Time

We try to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours. Please be patient as we're a small team dedicated to making Tomo the best it can be!

Community Guidelines

When contacting us, please be respectful and provide as much detail as possible about any issues you're experiencing. This helps us help you faster!
